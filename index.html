The best King Cakes in New Orleans

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients in order to bring you the best King Cakes New Orleans has to offer.

Our King Cakes are made with only the best ingredients we can find and made with love in our own home in New Orleans.

Being sugar free, we use whole dates as our sweetener of choice. None of that processed sugar in any of its forms here! Please take a look at our available products to find out more about our amazing and unique flavors!

Products

Traditional

The King Cake is a New Orleans tradition that involves a pastry, a small plastic baby, and a party. The King Cake is baked with a small plastic baby hidden inside, the person who gets the slice with baby in it buys the next one.

Chocolate

Our chocolate King Cake is one of our best selling King Cakes that we offer and is one of our favorites as well. Baked with delicious gooey chocolate inside and drizzled with chocolate on the outside.

Strawberry

Our rich strawberry King Cake is brished with melted butter and filled with rich cream cheese filling, topped with sweet starberry jam and real strawberries. We finish each one with Mardi Gras sprinkles.
